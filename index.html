About ScholarGuide AI

Accessing scholarship information is a significant challenge for many students, especially those from rural and low-income backgrounds. ScholarGuide AI simplifies scholarship search and eligibility discovery with a conversational assistant that accepts voice or text in the student’s preferred language.

Voice & Text Interaction

Students can speak or type their queries, and the system converts speech into text for faster access.

Multilingual Support

The platform supports regional languages so students can interact comfortably in their native language.

Eligibility Recommendations

Intelligent NLP extracts qualifications, income, location, and category details to match students with relevant scholarships.

Key Features

  • Automated scholarship discovery from a structured database
  • Simple explanations of eligibility criteria, deadlines, documents, and benefits
  • Speech recognition and multilingual conversational UI
  • Personalized recommendations based on academic, financial, and demographic details
  • Designed for rural and low-income students with limited digital literacy

How It Works

1. Capture Query

Speech recognition converts spoken questions into text and supports regional languages.

2. Extract Details

NLP extracts key student information such as qualification, academic score, income, and location.

3. Match Scholarships

The system compares student details against scholarship eligibility rules and returns the best matches.

4. Explain & Guide

Students receive clear guidance on required documents, deadlines, benefits, and application steps.
